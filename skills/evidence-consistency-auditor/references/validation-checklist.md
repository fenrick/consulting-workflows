# Evidence Consistency Audit Validation Checklist

## Audit coverage checks

- All claim-bearing artifacts in scope were listed.
- Every flagged issue names the conflicting sources.
- Supersession and date/version checks were performed.
- Weak-support items are separated from true contradictions.

## Resolution checks

- Closed issues include a rationale.
- Unresolved issues remain open in `tracking/open-questions.md`.
- Accepted residual risks were logged in `tracking/decision-log.md`.
- Revisions triggered a re-audit where required.

## Writing checks

- The artifact was built through skeleton, bullets, short prose, and flow passes.
- Language is plain, neutral, and non-accusatory.
- Humanisation removed awkward phrasing without changing the evidence position.
