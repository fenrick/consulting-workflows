# Presentation Storyboard Validation Checklist

## Slide logic checks

- Every slide has one clear audience takeaway.
- Opening, analysis, and closing sequences are explicit.
- Transitions connect the sequence cleanly.
- Duplicate, unsupported, or decorative slides were removed.

## Evidence checks

- Each slide has an evidence payload or an explicit gap note.
- High-risk claims are not stranded in presenter notes without support.
- Unsupported slides are recorded in `tracking/open-questions.md`.

## Writing checks

- The artifact was built through skeleton, bullets, short notes, and flow passes.
- Language is neutral and simple.
- Humanisation removed machine phrasing without changing message logic.
