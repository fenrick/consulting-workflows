# Workshop Write-up Validation Checklist

## Evidence checks

- Major discussion themes cite transcript or artifact support.
- Decisions are supported by evidence, not inferred.
- Action items include owners or timing only when evidenced.
- Disagreement or unresolved points were preserved where relevant.

## Structure checks

- Purpose, participants, session flow, decisions, actions, and unresolved issues are present.
- Multiple sessions are labelled clearly when combined.
- Evidence map and write-up tell the same story.

## Writing checks

- The artifact was built through skeleton, evidence bullets, short paragraphs, and flow passes.
- Language is neutral and simple.
- Humanisation removed machine habits without changing the factual record.
