# Input Evidence Catalog Validation Checklist

## Purpose

Use this checklist to confirm that intake outputs are factual, complete enough for downstream use, and explicit about what remains unknown.

## How to use this checklist

Run it after the first full inventory and again before handoff.

- Check inventory coverage first.
- Check fact extraction discipline second.
- Check writing quality last.

## Inventory checks

- Every input item in scope appears in the source register.
- Archives and nested folders are mapped in `inputs/processed/document-map.md`.
- Provenance, date, and author signals were checked where available.
- Apparent supersession relationships are recorded.

## Fact extraction checks

- Review notebook entries are factual, not interpretive.
- Unknowns are marked explicitly.
- Contradictions or metadata gaps were logged in `tracking/open-questions.md`.
- Contextual files are not presented as controlled evidence without a note.

## Writing checks

- The artifact was built through inventory skeleton, factual bullets, short prose, and flow passes.
- Language is plain and neutral.
- Humanisation did not introduce interpretation.
