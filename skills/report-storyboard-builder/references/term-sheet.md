# Report Storyboard Term Sheet

- `section purpose`: why the section exists in the report
- `key claim`: the main point the section must establish
- `proof obligation`: what the section must prove or document
- `practical implication`: why the section matters to the reader
- `exclusion`: content that belongs elsewhere
- `drafting order`: the recommended writing sequence

## Usage rules

- Prefer `proof obligation` over `support needed` when the evidence burden matters.
- Prefer `exclusion` over `out of scope` for section-level boundaries.
- Keep `section purpose` visible in planning notes and templates.
